What does Alfred offer?

Economy

Alfred has an economy system that is extremely simple but yet awesome! Use your tokens to get addicted to our gambling system, rob your friends to gain more tokens! Beg to gain tokens and earn more with those. We are planning on releasing a lot more updates for Alfred's economy so stay tuned!

Fun

We have an extremely awesome wide array of fun commands, from memes to news to eightball you can use Alfred and you won't realize how fast time passes! Our funny memes will hook you for hours and our extremely hot news will surprise the hell out of you. Since we are still in Beta stages you can expect a lot more to come.

Games

While our command list isn't stacked with games right now, Alfred will soon have plenty of games for you and your community to enjoy, they include but not limit to tic tac toe, connect4 and many other games. Just like the other sections we shall keep updating and improving to give you the best experience.
